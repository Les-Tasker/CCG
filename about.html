CC GEOTECHNICAL LTD (CCG) is a multi-disciplinary consultancy operating throughout the UK, Ireland and overseas, providing specialist technical support services to the construction industry.

Managed by a team of well qualified engineers, scientists and technicians, CCG specialises in the planning and execution of land and marine geotechnical and geoenvironmental investigations, subsidence investigations, structural investigations, highway pavement assessments etc.

Since its establishment in 1990, the company has undertaken over 7000 commissions relating to highway and major infrastructure schemes, residential and commercial property development, railway and rail station projects, land transactions, industrial developments, dock construction and quayside redevelopments, landfill construction etc..

Field services are supported by a comprehensive range of in-house UKAS testing services relating to soils, fresh and hardened concrete, bituminous materials, aggregates and construction materials, and soil and groundwater contamination land through our associated MCERTS / UKAS testing partner.

Our in-house support services further extend to cover long term instrumentation and monitoring programmes in the engineering and environmental contexts, covering settlement and movements of buildings and earthworks, landfill gas monitoring, leachate sampling and analysis etc.

This comprehensive capability allows CCG to offer a holistic service, addressing the full spectrum of structural, geotechnical and contaminated land issues.

The company has a reputation for resilience and reliability in meeting the requirements of both client and project in terms of programme, cost and quality, and since its inception has developed a portfolio of long standing blue chip clients including public authorities, utility providers, major house builders, structural and civil consultancies, insurance companies, contractors, financial institutions etc.

The firm is ISO 9001 quality assured, and is certified under the Link-up scheme for rail projects. Our commitment to operating to the highest standards of safety is evidenced by our status as a CHAS and Constructionline approved company.
